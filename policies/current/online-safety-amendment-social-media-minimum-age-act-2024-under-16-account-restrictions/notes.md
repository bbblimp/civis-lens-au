## Agenda Queue Notes

- Focus the review on the current federal under-16 social media account restriction framework and its implementation, not a general review of all online safety law.
- Distinguish the enacted statutory obligations from implementation guidance, platform-specific determinations, age-assurance technology claims, and contested empirical claims about youth mental health or online harm.
- Treat child safety, privacy, freedom of expression, enforcement feasibility, competition effects, parental choice, and impacts on vulnerable young people as separate evaluation dimensions.
